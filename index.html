About Us

Established in 2017 by Mrs. Preeti Verma in assistance of Mr. Rakesh Kumar Shrivastava, Absolute HR Solution and Consultancy specializes in labor law compliance, payroll processing, and end-to-end HR services. We simplify HR management and guide businesses through complex compliance landscapes with precision and confidentiality.

Our Services

What it means: Labour law consulting involves guiding businesses through complex employment laws and regulations to ensure compliance and protect employee rights.

What we provide: We offer expert advice and documentation support on Central and State labour laws including Minimum Wages, Factories Act, Industrial Disputes, and more.

How it benefits you: Avoid penalties and legal conflicts while ensuring a fair and safe workplace that complies with government mandates.

What it means: Payroll processing is the administration of employee salaries, deductions, taxes, and net payments on a monthly basis.

What we provide: We handle end-to-end payroll tasks including salary calculations, TDS, PF/ESI deductions, payslips, and statutory filings.

How it benefits you: Reduce administrative burden, eliminate errors, and ensure employees are paid correctly and on time, every time.

What it means: Compensation and benefits involve structuring employee salaries and perks to align with company goals and employee expectations.

What we provide: We help design competitive pay structures, incentive plans, and benefits packages suited to your business needs.

How it benefits you: Attract and retain top talent while boosting employee satisfaction and staying compliant with tax and labour laws.

What it means: Statutory compliance refers to adhering to legal requirements like Provident Fund, ESI, and other labour-related laws.

What we provide: We manage documentation, submissions, inspections, and deadlines for all applicable statutory contributions and returns.

How it benefits you: Ensure legal compliance, avoid fines and penalties, and build a trustworthy reputation with employees and regulators.

What it means: Admin HR consultancy supports the routine HR functions such as record keeping, onboarding, and HR policy management.

What we provide: We assist with documentation, HRIS usage, employee records, benefits administration, and process audits.

How it benefits you: Streamline HR operations, maintain compliance, and focus on strategic HR goals without worrying about administrative overload.

What it means: Manpower placement involves sourcing, screening, and recruiting candidates for open positions within your organization.

What we provide: We offer customized hiring solutions for both core and non-core sectors, delivering recruitment services for entry to senior-level roles across MNCs, SMEs, FMCGs, IT sector and Engineering industries. Our offerings include permanent, contractual, and project-based staffing.

How it benefits you: Reduce hiring time, improve candidate quality, and focus on core business functions while we handle the talent pipeline.

What it means: Factory license consulting ensures that your industrial operations comply with legal requirements set by the Factories Act.

What we provide: We manage the complete process of applying for new licenses, renewals, modifications, and inspections.

How it benefits you: Operate without legal interruptions and gain the trust of stakeholders through transparent compliance.

What it means: Businesses must comply with pollution control laws related to air and water usage/disposal, regulated by environmental boards.

What we provide: We facilitate clearances, renewals, and reporting with the State Pollution Control Boards to ensure full environmental compliance.

How it benefits you: Avoid environmental penalties, reduce operational risks, and enhance your brand’s sustainability credentials.
